Frequently Asked Questions

  • A VPN allows you to change your IP address so that you appear to be located in a different country.
  • Unfortunately, some VPN's servers are known to Netflix and they block them from accessing their site. Contact your VPN provider as they will often change the location of their servers to avoid this problem. Alternatively try a different VPN provider.
  • Try logging out of your account and then clearing the cookies in your browser. Now, while still connected to your VPN go to Netflix and login again. This often solves the problem as the previously stored cookies contained data with your actual location.
